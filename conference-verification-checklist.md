# Conference Verification Checklist

Before submitting or paying, verify the following directly from the official organizer page or contact email:

- Exact conference name
- Organizer name
- Event date
- Venue city and full address
- Abstract submission deadline
- Student registration fee
- Whether student presenters are accepted
- Whether the conference provides certificates
- Whether proceedings or abstracts are published
- Refund policy
- Contact phone and email

Important note:

Do not rely only on third-party conference listing websites. If venue/date details differ across sites, contact the organizer before payment.
